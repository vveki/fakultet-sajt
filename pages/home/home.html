Shark

Sharks are ancient marine creatures and vital to ecosystems. Most are harmless to humans but face threats from fishing. Protecting them is crucial for ocean health.

Dolphin

Dolphins are smart and social marine mammals, known for their playfulness and echolocation skills. They are vital to the ocean's balance but face threats from pollution and fishing. Protecting them ensures healthier seas.

Turtle

Turtles are ancient reptiles with protective shells and long migration routes. They are key to ocean ecosystems but endangered by habitat loss and pollution. Conservation is essential to their survival.
